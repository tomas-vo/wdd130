"Santiago, Chile is the capital and largest city of Chile, located in a valley surrounded by the Andes Mountains. It is the country's economic, cultural, and political center, offering a mix of modern skyscrapers and historic architecture. The city has a Mediterranean climate with hot summers and mild winters. Visitors can explore attractions like Plaza de Armas, Cerro San Cristóbal, and La Moneda Palace. Santiago also has a vibrant food scene, museums, and nearby vineyards, making it a great destination for tourism and business."
